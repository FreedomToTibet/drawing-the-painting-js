Frequently Asked Questions

Answering any of your questions

How long does it take to complete the order?

The time to complete an order depends on three factors: the complexity of the painting, the style of execution, and the size. The average time to complete an order is 7 days excluding delivery time. In some cases, at the customer's request, the deadline can be reduced to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to make changes to the painting, make adjustments, it may take additional time, which is not taken into account in the agreed completion time.

Will I be able to see the painting before receiving it?

Yes. Upon completion, we send you a digital photo of your painting by email so that you can make corrections and adjustments if necessary. You can also see the painting "live" in our showroom. Inform your manager about this.

What format should the photos be in?

We work with all known image formats! Through the website, you can upload JPEG, TIFF, PNG files up to 50 megabytes in size. You must own the copyrights or have permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), you can use the FTP uploader. If you want to take a photo from a paper photograph (not digital), send it to us, we will scan, process it, and return the photo together with your order!

Can I give special instructions to the artist?

When placing your order on the website, you can add your comments in a special text field. You can also: combine objects from different photos into one or remove objects from photos, change the background, change clothes, outfits, make a color picture from a black and white photo, and also order a picture from a damaged or old photo.
